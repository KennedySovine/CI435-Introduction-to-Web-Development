What is Feedback?

Feedback is information about your work/performance, which can be used as a basis for improvement.

Feedback: "Information or statements of opinion about something, such as a new product, that can tell you if it is successful or liked."

According to Cambridge dictionary:

Feedback is essentially opinions you get from others. Typically, it is given by peers and trusted

How I Have Developed Skills in Feedback

I have developed skills in feedback in 2 ways:

  1. Giving feedback
  2. Recieving feedback

Both of these have been developed as my time as a course representitive. Every few weeks, there are student staff fourms where the course representives meet wit hthe staff and bring up any concerns about the course. This can be about equipment, the modules, or even about the entire course in general.

Through these staff fourms, I have presented feedback to instructors on how my course-mates view the modules that we must take. This requires a lot of thought and skill as giving feedback incorrectly can sometimes achieve the opposite of what you want or think would be beneficial.

I've also recieved a lot of feedback for CI435 from my boyfriend as he's helped me develop my website. He's given me feedback on good or bad design decisions I've made while creating, and I've done the same for him.
